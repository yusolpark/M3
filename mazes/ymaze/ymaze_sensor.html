Y-maze asseses short-term spatial memory by quantifying “spontaneous alternation” between maze arms. The Y-maze consists of three arms angled 120 degrees apart.

Assembly Instructions

1. Print out the parts
2. Place the triangle floor at the center
3. Add 4 floors onto each side of the triangle floor

Y-maze Images

Parts

1 x triangle floors (with junctions)

12 x square floors

27 x wall

6 x 2-way junctions (right angle)

18 x 2-way junctions (stright)
